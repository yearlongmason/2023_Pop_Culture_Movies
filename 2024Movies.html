Movie Awards of 2023

2024 is shaping up to be a very exciting year for movies. With 2023 coming out with a strong comeback for movies after COVID, it's looking like 2024 will meet the standard that 2023 set! Once again, I talked with my local theater's movie coordinator, Zackary Quick, about some movies coming out in 2024 that are expected to make the biggest impact on pop culture as a whole. On this page, I showcase a couple of those movies and talk a little bit about what makes each of them special. For more information about each movie, click on the title shown above the poster! That will redirect you to the Letterboxd page for each movie, which has all the information you could look for and more!

Already, this movie has made a huge impact on pop culture through social media and memes! Now fans are excited for the next installment in the Dune franchise: Dune: Messiah.

The Fall Guy

The Fall Guy is a highly anticipated action-romantic comedy that's been in the works for a long time and is finally coming out in early May! This movie has a star-studded cast, primarily featuring Ryan Gosling and Emily Blunt. This movie has already taken over social media through the classic "literally me" Ryan Gosling meme, where people try to relate to Ryan Gosling's characters through things that they think are relatable or wish they could do. Anticipation for this movie has been growing for a couple years, and based on the trailers, I don't blame them! This will be one movie this summer you won't want to miss.

Monkey Man

Monkey Man is an action movie that was directed, written by, and starring Dev Patel, who is known for his work in The Green Knight, Lion, and Hotel Mumbai. The most interesting story about this movie, in my opinion, is actually about the conditions under which it was made. According to the mastermind behind the movie (Dev Patel), production was a complete disaster. This is because they started filming in March of 2020. Sound familiar? Because COVID hit just as they were starting production, they had enormous struggles getting almost anything done on set. But because of how much the team cared about the movie, they pushed through and made it work. Because of how much care was put into this movie, people are excited to see just how special it is!

Inside Out 2

Inside Out 2 is an obvious one. Inside Out is one of Pixar's most popular movies; it's no wonder fans were begging for a sequel! The plot of this movie revolves around the main character growing up and developing new types of feelings, such as anxiety. This has sparked a lot of jokes on social media, with people being able to relate to how anxiety is represented in this movie. This movie is exactly what people have been looking for since the first one came out nearly 10 years ago, in 2015.

Bad Boys: Ride or Die

Bad Boys: Ride or Die is the fourth movie in the Bad Boys franchise about two cops, starring Will Smith and Martin Lawrence. These movies are especially popular with people who were around when the first movies were just coming out in the 1990's and 2000's, but they also gained the ability to attract new fans when they came out with the third installment, Bad Boys for Life, in 2020, hoping to gain attention from a newer audience. Fans of the original and the new ones all over are excited for this movie to hit theaters this June 7th so they can go on another ride along with their favorite Miami police officer, Mike Lowrey!
