Barbie

Potentially the most popular movie of the year, Barbie was absolutely huge! One of the big reasons this was one of the most popular movies of the year was because of who was involved in the project. First, it was written and directed by Greta Gerwig, who had already directed other popular movies such as Lady Bird and Little Women. Second, it had a really star-studded cast with actors like Margot Robbie, Ryan Gosling, Will Ferrel, Michael Cera, and cameos from people like John Cena! All of these people are huge names in the world of pop culture, and their appearances alone in a movie are bound to draw a crowd! Aside from the people involved in the project, the whole movie revolved around the nostalgia of the concept of Barbie. The doll everyone used to play with when they were younger. In these ways, the movie leveraged pop culture to really draw a crowd to the theaters! As a result, this movie was turned into memes and gifs, and the music was used in all sorts of social media, making it really have a huge impact on pop culture. Never had the theaters been more pink than the month Barbie came out!

Oppenheimer

Oppenheimer was a fantastic movie that told the story of J. Robert Oppenheimer and the creation of the atomic bomb. Along with all the other movies on this list, Oppenheimer had a star-studded cast, including actors like Cillian Murphy, Emily Blunt, Matt Damon, and Robert Downey Jr. Not only did it have a star-studded cast, but it was also directed by Christopher Nolan, who has directed many other famous movies such as The Dark Knight trilogy, Interstellar, Inception, and The Prestige. One of the biggest splashes this movie made in the world of pop culture was Oppenheimer's shared meme with Barbie. Of course I'm talking about "Barbenheimer." Both Barbie and Oppenheimer opened in theaters on July 21st, 2023. As you probably know, if you know anything about either of the movies, it is that they have completely different tones. Oppenheimer is about creating a bomb during World War II, while Barbie is about dolls that come to life. This, of course, sparked the idea in people's minds of doing a Barbie/Oppenheimer double feature. This was huge in pop culture for the months leading up to and following the release of Barbie and Oppenheimer. I would even go so far as to argue that "Barbenheimer" was one of the biggest pop culture events of the year!

Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse

Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse is a continuation of Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse and is one of two parts. It follows Miles Morales as one of many Spider-Men across the multiverse and largely focuses on comic book adaptations of Spider-Man. One of the things that made this movie really popular was its animation style, in that it was animated to look as if it were a comic book, which is really something that was revolutionary when the first Spider-Verse movie came out. This movie brought in a couple stars to really sell their movie, like Oscar Isaac, Jason Schwartzman, and Jake Johnson! On top of its cast and incredible animation style, it's also renowned for its incredible soundtrack. This movie showed up in a lot of forms in memes across social media, specifically with Oscar Isaac's character, Spider-Man 2099. This movie also pulled a twist toward the end when it revealed itself to be the first part of a two-part story, which both excited and let down audiences. On one hand, it got people all excited for another Spider-Verse movie to come out, but on the other hand, it let people down who were looking for an ending to the story that got started. The first Spider-Verse movie in 2018 set a high standard for the future of Spider-Verse movies, and that expectation was certainly exceeded with this movie, which has set an even higher precedent for Spider-Man: Beyond the Spider-Verse, which is expected to release sometime in 2025!

The Super Mario Bros. Movie

The Super Mario Bros. Movie was a huge hit with a lot of audiences, but not so much with the critics, so much so that on rotten tomatoes, it got a 59% with critics and a 95% audience score! A lot of this is definitely due to the nostalgia of a lot of 90's and 2000's kids reliving their childhood memories of playing the game! Of course, there was also Bowser's song, Peaches, that managed to really sneak its way into the hearts of audiences and into pop culture! For months after the release date of the movie, the song was in people's social media feeds and even on the radio! The movie also had a pretty huge cast of stars, including Chris Pratt, Charlie Day, Jack Black, and Seth Rogan! One of the more controversial topics in relation to the movie was the casting of Chris Pratt as Mario. People's biggest issue with the casting was that there wasn't a real attempt to recreate the classic Mario voice, and instead it just sounded like Chris Pratt's regular voice. Unfortunately, this trend doesn't seem to be stopping, as Chris Pratt was also cast as the voice of Garfield in The Garfield Movie, releasing in 2024, and is once again just using his regular voice. Overall, The Super Mario Bros. Movie was a pretty huge hit with audiences, largely due to the nostalgia of reliving the feeling of playing the Super Mario Bros. game when they were younger, and it made a pretty big wave in pop culture due to its popularity and hilarious songs!

Mission Impossible: Dead Reckoning

Mission Impossible: Dead Reckoning was a pretty pleasant surprise for critics, but it continued its great reputation with audiences. The Mission Impossible movies for the last two decades have really been on the uptrend. Since the disaster of Mission Impossible II, the movies have only gotten better, with Mission Impossible: Fallout being arguably the best in the franchise! To the excitement of the viewers, this movie didn't disappoint! From beginning to end, people were kept on the edge of their seats for the classic Mission Impossible thrill! Some of the excitement of the Mission Impossible movies is that for every movie, Tom Cruise will perform some crazy, dangerous, physics-defying stunt and baffle his fans! In the past, he has been free climbing in Mission Impossible II, learned how to hold his breath for six minutes, and even hung off the side of a plane as it took off in Mission Impossible: Rogue Nation! Dead Reckoning was no different, as Tom Cruise took to the skies as he drove a motorcycle off a cliff and used a parachute to land on a train! This movie kept up the standard of Mission Impossible stunts and really pushed the expectations and boundaries of action movies!

The Hunger Games: The Ballad of Songbirds & Snakes

The Hunger Games: The Ballad of Songbirds & Snakes is a movie that serves as a prequel to the original Hunger Games movies! It follows a young President Snow and focuses on the story of how he came to power. The movie was directed by Francis Lawrence, who had also directed the last three Hunger Games movies: Catching Fire and Mockingjay Parts 1 and 2. This movie was pretty nostalgic for a lot of people who were either fans of the books or the movies. One of the biggest impacts this movie had in the pop culture world was how the actors were received and the impacts they made on social media. For months after the release of the movie, there were posts on Instagram thirsting for young President Snow. One of the interesting things about this movie's advertising is that they really used social media platforms to the max to advertise their movie by having cast members make TikTok and Instagram reels about the movie and the original movies! I think this movie is one of the first of many that will really start to rely on social media as an advertising platform!
